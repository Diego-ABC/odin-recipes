Fried Salami, Domican deli style

Description

This is my personal, public recipe for ok fried salami.

It's still under development.

Ingredients

  • 1 salami sausage thing

Steps

  1. slice out aas many slices of salami you want, but they need to all fit on the pan surface. Try to keep it under 1cm think
  2. heat pan to really hot
  3. place slices flat on the pan
  4. let slices cook until dark on one side. medium flame for about 5 minutes
  5. flip all the slices and wait another 5 minutes
  6. salami is ready. Eat alone or with your dog
