Eggs Over Medium

Description

This is my personal, private recipe for perfect fried eggs. Goes good with homefries

Follow along to get a glimpse at greatness.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tspn salt
  • 1/2 tspn ground black pepper

Steps

  1. heat pan until boiling
  2. place 1/2 tablespoon of butter on the pan.
  3. crack 2 eggs over the pan
  4. place sprinkle salt and pepper over the eggs
  5. cover pan
  6. let eggs cook over small flame for 5 minutes
  7. eggs done
  8. eat off the pan, or mix with homefries
