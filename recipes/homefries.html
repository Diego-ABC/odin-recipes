Homefries

Description

This is my personal, private recipe for perfect homefries.

Follow along to get a glimpse at greatness.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tspn salt
  • 1 tspn ground black pepper
  • 1 idaho potato

Steps

  1. wash your potato of all dirt and whatever
  2. cut up the potato into bits of whatever size you want (usually < 1cu in)
  3. place the bits of potato in a bowl of water
  4. microwave the bowl for about a minute and a half
  5. drain the water out of the bowl
  6. heat pan until water boils away instantly
  7. place 1 tablespoon of butter on the pan.
  8. spread the melting butter across the surface of the pan
  9. place the potato in the butter
  10. mix the potato throughly in the butter to coat it evenly
  11. add the salt and pepper evenly over the potato
  12. cover the pan and let the potato cook over a small-medium flame for 12-15 minutes
  13. potato is done, eat off the pan
